About Sri Lanka Railway Department

Connecting the island with reliable and efficient rail services since 1864.

Welcome to Sri Lanka Railways

The Sri Lanka Railway Department is the national railway operator in Sri Lanka, providing passenger and freight services across the island. With a rich history spanning over 150 years, we are committed to delivering safe, reliable, and comfortable travel experiences for all.

Our network spans over 1,500 kilometers, connecting major cities, towns, and rural areas. Whether you're traveling for business or leisure, Sri Lanka Railways offers a convenient and affordable way to explore the island.

Our History

Sri Lanka Railways was established in 1864 during the British colonial era. The first railway line connected Colombo with Kandy, marking the beginning of a new era in transportation. Over the years, we have expanded our network and modernized our services to meet the growing needs of our passengers.

1864

First railway line opened between Colombo and Kandy.

1950

Introduction of diesel locomotives.

2020

Modernization of railway infrastructure and services.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide safe, efficient, and sustainable rail transport services that meet the needs of our passengers and contribute to the economic development of Sri Lanka.
