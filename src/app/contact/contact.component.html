Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a train ticket?

You can book a train ticket online through our website or mobile app. Simply select your destination, date, and class, and proceed to payment.

 What should I do if I lose my ticket?

If you lose your ticket, please contact our customer support team immediately. You may need to provide your booking reference number and identification.

 Can I cancel or change my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or change your booking online. Please note that cancellation fees may apply depending on the time of cancellation.

 How do I check train schedules?

You can check train schedules on our website or mobile app. Simply enter your departure and destination stations to view available trains.
